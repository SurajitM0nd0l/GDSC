Google developer Student Club(GDSC) is
a initiave of Google Developer for
university students to enhance their skills

Concept of GDSC

The GDSC program is a grassroots channel
through which Google provides
development skills, while enhancing the
quality of being and enjoying the events.

Why GDSC?

For student to learn development skills,
slove problems through technology and
inspire them to be world class developers
and changemakers.

Target audience

GDSC activities targeted at University
students and any others including
members who want to learn development
skills & work to solve real-life problem.

Opportunities GDSCs provide students with

Grow their knowledge on developer technologies and more through peer to peerworkshop and event.
Gain revelent industry experience by solving problem fro local organizations with technology based solution.
Showcase their prototypes ans solution to their local community leader.
Getting inspriation to become world class developers and changemakers from sharing others success stories.
